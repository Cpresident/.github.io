MG相关micro RNA（2019-06-09）

目标

  • 快速查阅一下MG的相关micro RNA，最特异的是不是Micro RNA 150p-5p和21-5p和let-7?
  • 这些microRNA是和T细胞还是B细胞相关？疾病的特异性能到啥程度？

结论

【总结】

  • MG相关micro RNA的研究有两大类，一是对血样分析，二是对胸腺组织分析。而结论也有两个方向，前者偏向于micro RNA对炎症通路的影响，而后者偏向于micro RNA对胸腺发生发育的影响（以T细胞为主）。

【最特异的是不是Micro RNA 150p-5p和21-5p和let-7？疾病的特异性能到啥程度？】

  • Micro RNA 150p-5p可能是最特异的，它与AChR+ MG 临床症状+体内检测都相关联。21-5p支持的文献没有150p-5p多。let-7有MuSK+ MG的倾向。
  • 若深入进去看文献，可以发现micro RNA的特异性能用其调控机制解释。miR-150-5p对胸腺的影响导致其与胸腺增生、胸腺瘤MG相关。miR-21-5p因和prednisone 用量相关，故推测其机制为炎症通路调控。let家族的MUSK MG倾向原因未知。
  • 故疾病的特异性能细化到MG的内部几种分型。目前文献最支持的是miR-150-5p与胸腺增生型/EOMG的关系。
  • 若是量化Micro RNA 与MG分型的特异性差异则需要做Meta了

【这些microRNA是和T细胞还是B细胞相关？】

  • 这些microRNA与T、B都相关，但以T细胞为主。

Circulating microRNAs as potential biomarkers in myasthenia gravis patients.

综述

  • Recent studies in AChR+ MG revealed elevated levels of the immuno-miRNAs miR-150-5p and miR-21-5p. Of particular importance, levels of miR-150-5p were lower in immunosuppressed patients and in patients with clinical improvement following thymectomy.
  • In MuSK+ MG, another profile of circulating miRNAs was found, including upregulation of the let-7 family of miRNAs.

  • Of particular importance, levels of miR-150-5p were lower in immunosuppressed patients and in patients with clinical improvement following thymectomy. In MuSK+ MG, another profile of circulating miRNAs was found, including upregulation of the let-7 family of miRNAs.

    Thymectomy lowers the myasthenia gravis biomarker miR-150-5p.

本试验测了两个marker（miR-150-5p and miR-21-5p），Thymectomy 只降低了 miR-150-5p，但prednisone 剂量却与miR-21-5p相关

  • Levels of miR-21-5p displayed a negative correlation with the prednisone dose within the prednisone-only group (p ≤ 0.001).
  • Patients treated with thymectomy had lower levels of miR-150-5p at 24 months, both compared with baseline values (p = 0.0011) and the prednisone group (p = 0.04).

Causes and Consequences of miR-150-5p Dysregulation in Myasthenia Gravis.

miR-150–5p与胸腺增生相关

  • Increased expression of miR-150–5p associated with thymic hyperplasia in MG.

T细胞还是B细胞相关？

Causes and Consequences of miR-150-5p Dysregulation in Myasthenia Gravis.

假说：CD4+ T cells释放的miR-150-5p对循环的PBMCs产生影响

  • The microRNA miR-150-5p has been previously characterized as a biomarker in MG due to its increase in the serum of patients and its decrease after thymectomy, correlated with an improvement of symptoms.

  • Here, we investigated the causes and consequences of the miR-150 increase in the serum of early-onset MG patients. We observed that miR-150 expression was upregulated in MG thymuses in correlation with the presence of thymic B cells and showed by in situ hybridization experiments, that miR-150 was mainly expressed by cells of the mantle zone of GCs.

  • We observed that miR-150 was down-regulated, especially in CD4+ T cells compared to controls. (These results suggest that the increased serum levels of miR-150 could result from a release from activated peripheral CD4+ T cells.)

Immature Exosomes Derived from MicroRNA-146a Overexpressing Dendritic Cells Act as Antigen-Specific Therapy for Myasthenia Gravis.

EAMG中的microRNA-146a可以影响 T helper cell profiles

  • Results showed that exosomes from microRNA-146a overexpressing DCs expressed decreased levels of CD80 and CD86. In experimental autoimmune MG, exosomes from microRNA-146a overexpressing DCs suppressed ongoing clinical MG in mice and altered T helper cell profiles from Th1/Th17 to Th2/Treg both in serum and spleen, and the therapeutic effects of those exosomes were antigen-specific and partly dose dependent. Myasthenia gravis: from autoantibodies to therapy.

综述

  • MicroRNAs (miRNAs), key gene expression regulators, play a role in autoimmune disorders via modulation of innate and adaptive immunity [34]. Their dysregulation might underlie pathological changes occurring in hyperplastic myasthenia gravis thymus as recently highlighted by Cron et al. [35], who observed a down-regulation of miR-7
    and an up-regulation of miR-125, targeting the germinal centre-related CCL21 and Foxp3, respectively [35]. Another study showed a miR-548k downregulation in hyperplastic myasthenia gravis thymuses and its ability to regulate B-cell-attracting chemokine CXCL13 expression [36], further supporting miRNA contribution to intra-thymic myasthenia gravis pathogenesis.

miR-20b Inhibits T Cell Proliferation and Activation via NFAT Signaling Pathway in Thymoma-Associated Myasthenia Gravis.

miR-20b抑制了Thymoma-Associated MG的T细胞增殖

  • Expression of miR-20b was downregulated in samples of thymoma tissues and serum from patients with thymoma-associated myasthenia gravis.
  • In addition, T cell proliferation and activation were inhibited by ectopic overexpression of miR-20b, which led to increased T cell proliferation and activation.

MiR-15a contributes abnormal immune response in myasthenia gravis by targeting CXCL10.

MiR-15a通过CXCL10介导T细胞调控

  • Increasing miR-15a expression could reduce CXCL10 expression and alleviate the abnormal T cells activation in immune response, while decreasing miR-15a expression could activate immune response abnormally.
  • Take together, our data suggest that decreased miR-15a expression facilitates proinflammatory cytokines production and contributes to immune response at least in part via regulating CXCL10 expression in MG.

Aberrant decrease of microRNA19b regulates TSLP expression and contributes to Th17 cells development in myasthenia gravis related thymomas.

microRNA-19b抑制 thymic stromal lymphopoietin (TSLP)表达来影响T细胞发育

  • This study indicated that the elevation of microRNA-19b suppressed TSLP expression and then influenced T cell development in thymomatous MG.

Altered let-7 expression in Myasthenia gravis and let-7c mediated regulation of IL-10 by directly targeting IL-10 in Jurkat cells.

let-7c通过Jurkat cells (T细胞)影响IL-10

  • Further experiments revealed that induced levels of IL-10 were inversely related to let-7c levels.
  • We also showed that let-7c could regulate IL-10 expression in Jurkat cells.

Identification of novel MicroRNA signatures linked to experimental autoimmune myasthenia gravis pathogenesis: down-regulated miR-145 promotes pathogenetic Th17 cell response.

下调miR-145 增加Th17 cell response

  • Down-regulation of miR-145 expression was confirmed in PBMC and CD4+CD25- T cells (T effector cells) from both EAMG rats and MG patients by real-time PCR.
  • Finally, we observed that administration of lentiviral-miR-145 decreased the severity of ongoing, established EAMG with decreased IL-17 production, and also decreased serum anti-AChR IgG levels.

Silencing miR-146a influences B cells and ameliorates experimental autoimmune myasthenia gravis.

miR-146a 调控MG的B细胞

  • Our data also demonstrated that silencing miR-146a with its inhibitor AntagomiR-146a effectively ameliorated clinical myasthenic symptoms in mice with ongoing experimental autoimmune myasthenia gravis.

  • Furthermore, multiple defects were observed after miR-146a was knocked down in B cells, including decreased anti-R97-116 antibody production and class switching, reduced numbers of plasma cells, memory B cells and B-1 cells, and weakened activation of B cells.

Delivery of an miR155 inhibitor by anti-CD20 single-chain antibody into B cells reduces the acetylcholine receptor-specific autoantibodies and ameliorates experimental autoimmune myasthenia gravis.

抑制B细胞内的MicroRNA-155 可以减轻EAMG

  • In this study, we observed up-regulation of miR155 in the peripheral blood mononuclear cells (PBMCs) of patients with myasthenia gravis (MG), and miR155 was also up-regulated in torpedo acetylcholine receptor (T-AChR)-stimulated B cells.
  • Our results demonstrated that silencing of miR155 by its inhibitor impaired the B cell-activating factor (BAFF)-R-related signalling pathway and reduced the translocation of nuclear factor (NF)-κB into the nucleus.
  • Additionally, AChR-specific autoantibodies were reduced, which may be related to the altered amounts of marginal zone B cells and memory B cells in the spleens of EAMG mice.

Altered expression of miR-146a in myasthenia gravis.

miR-146a 与AchR specific B cells

  • We conclude that abnormal expression/regulation of miR-146a may play an important role in the regulation of AchR specific B cells and contribute to the pathogenesis of MG.

micro RNA影响炎症通路

miR-15b is Downregulated in Myasthenia Gravis Patients and Directly Regulates the Expression of Interleukin-15 (IL-15) in Experimental Myasthenia Gravis Mice.

miR-15b通过IL-15影响炎症反应

  • we confirmed that miR-15b knockdown could increase IL-15 expression in healthy mice, while miR-15b overexpression could inhibit IL-15 expression in EAMG mice.

Decreased microRNA miR-181c expression in peripheral blood mononuclear cells correlates with elevated serum levels of IL-7 and IL-17 in patients with myasthenia gravis.

Disease-specific signature of serum miR-20b and its targets IL-8 and IL-25, in myasthenia gravis patients.

miR-20b和QMGS关联（通过调控炎症因子IL-8 and IL-25）

  • Here, we report that miR-20b, an immune- and cancer-related miRNA, is decreased in the serum of MG patients and correlates negatively with QMGSs in the pretreatment stage.
