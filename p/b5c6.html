总结

  1. Treg既可以在胸腺生成，也可以被肿瘤或者体外诱导生成 iTregs【无论CD4^+^或CD8^+^】
  2. CD8^+^ Treg在免疫抑制的意义中似乎起着CD4^+^Treg的辅助作用
  3. 因为领域较新，对于CD8^+^ Treg的marker未有明确定义，广为使用的是CD8^+^ CD25^+^ FOXP3^+^ T cells

在MG的应用

  • 前提假说是：【异常胸腺增生导致的异常TCR repirtore】与【以HLA-B*08为代表的EOMG】相关
  • 作为结果可能是：
    1. 异常的CD8^+^ Treg可能在MG患者胸腺发育的时候即产生
    2. 异常的TCR repirtore诱导CD8^+^ 细胞在MG患者外周产生缺乏效力的CD8^+^ Treg？
    3. 以上二者都有
  • 如果要做CD8^+^ Treg的话，因为目前缺乏权威性的CD8^+^ Treg定义，建议先查资料全面考量其四五种marker.此外广为使用的CD8^+^ CD25^+^ FOXP3^+^ T cells版本是被有的文章反对的。

CD8+ Treg

CD8+ Treg cells suppress CD8+ T cell-responses by IL-10-dependent mechanism during H5N1 influenza virus infection. 2014

H5N1通过产生CD8+ Treg分泌IL-10抑制人体免疫反应

  • In addition to expressing CD25, the CD8(+) Foxp3(+) Treg cells showed a high level of GITR and produced IL-10.
  • In an adoptive transfer model, CD8(+) Treg cells suppressed CD8(+) T-cell responses and promoted H5N1 virus infection, resulting in enhanced mortality and increased virus load in the lung.
  • Here we found that in Foxp3-GFP transgenic mice, CD8(+) Foxp3(+) Treg cells, but not CD4(+) Foxp3(+) Treg cells, were remarkably induced during H5N1 infection.

Human and Mouse CD8(+)CD25(+)FOXP3(+) Regulatory T Cells at Steady State and during Interleukin-2 Therapy. 2015

本文介绍 CD8(+)CD25(+)FOXP3(+) Regulatory T Cells 在人类和小鼠中的性状特点

  • Among them, a small population of CD8(+)CD25(+)FOXP3(+) T cells is found both in mice and humans.

  • CD8(+) Tregs在人体的分布
    CD8(+) Tregs represent approximately 0.4 and 0.1% of peripheral blood T cells in healthy humans and mice, respectively. In mice, their frequencies are quite similar in lymph nodes (LNs) and the spleen, but two to threefold higher in Peyer patches and mesenteric LNs.

  • CD8(+) Tregs的抑制免疫效果可能比CD4 Tregs还要好
    In vitro, they suppress effector T cell proliferation as well as or even better than CD4(+) Tregs.

TGF-β1 contributes to CD8+ Treg induction through p38 MAPK signaling in ovarian cancer microenvironment. 2016

TGF-β1可以诱导CD8+ iTregs (CD8(+)CD25(+)FOXP3(+)) 的生成

  • TGF-β1 promotes the expansion of Treg populations by converting conventional CD4+CD25− T cells into Foxp3-expressing iTregs [20–22].
  • These data suggested that in vitro-induction of CD8+ Tregs depended in part on TGF-β1 activation of p38 MAPK signaling.
  • We have further revealed that human OC SKOV3 cells could convert CD8+ effector T cells into suppressive CD8+ Tregs in vitro [5].

CD8(+) Tregs promote GVHD prevention and overcome the impaired GVL effect mediated by CD4(+) Tregs in mice. 2016

从resting CD4(+) or CD8(+) T cells诱导induced Tregs (iTregs)，再用CD8(+) iTreg 抑制GVHD

  • Using similar polarizing conditions, we were able to generate 30–40% Foxp3+ CD8+ iTregs (Fig. S4A). These CD8+ iTregs displayed significantly higher levels of CD39+CD73+, CTLA-4, and granzyme B compared to nTregs and CD4+ iTregs (Fig. S4B), and were equally suppressive of allogeneic responses as CD4+ iTregs (Fig. S4C). Despite potent suppressive function in vitro, alloreactive CD8+ iTregs only moderately attenuated GVHD (Fig. S5A), but fully preserved the GVL effect against P815 tumor (Fig. S5B and C)

Revival of CD8+ Treg-mediated suppression. 2008

CD8+ Treg在胸腺生成，其机制提供了一种思路为【HLA-B*08—MG早期发病—胸腺增生】。

  • We also focus on a newly described subset, CD8alphaalpha(+)TCRalphabeta(+) Tregs, which in mice recognize a T-cell receptor-derived peptide in the context of the class Ib major histocompatibility complex molecule Qa-1.
  • CD8+ Treg 种类繁多

  • CD的比较 (Distinct features of CD4+FOXP3+ and CD8aa+TCRab+ Tregs)

  • 同CD4不同，TCR repertoire of CD8aa+TCRab+ Tregs非常狭小

    • 变态反应性脑脊髓炎：It is quite striking that the TCR peptide-reactive Qa-1-restricted CD8aa+TCRab+ Tregs in the experimental allergic encephalomyelitis (EAE) model predominantly express the TCR Vb6 gene segment [28].
    • 炎性肠病：CD8+ Tregs in the lamina propria of IBD patients were found to be predominantly TCR Vb5.1+ [19].
    • 上皮内淋巴细胞：Similarly, CD8aa+TCRab+ IELs have also been shown to have nonrandom Vb repertoires and preferential use ofTCR Vb6 and Vb11 gene segments [38].
  • 虽然MHC class Ib 对CD8aa+TCRab+ Tregs有限制性，但并不代表全部
    However, it should be emphasized that not all CD8+ Tregs are restricted by class Ib molecules, and class Ia restriction has also been demonstrated [48].
  • Development of CD8aa+TCRab+ Tregs—胸腺里某种MHC的限制性（这里是class Ib MHC molecules）
    self-reactive IELs and CD8ab+TCRab+ insulin-reactive T cells suggest an important role for the hematopoeitic cell–mediated thymic selection process (42,51).

  • Foxp3 intronic enhancer感应TCR和cytokine 起作用
    revealing a central role for a Foxp3 intronic enhancer that serves as a sensor of both TCR and cytokine signals and translates these inputs into increased Foxp3 transcription during Treg cell activation (8, 9).
