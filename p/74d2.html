MG危象+难治队列（Control+V0+Refractory）cytokines, T cells, and clinical scores

说明

  1. 因为wilcox.test和t.test结果差不多，所以所有数据统计全按照标准投稿要求计算的（即引入重复性p校正）

Cytokines

p.adjust.method = "hochberg", method = "wilcox.test", p=0.05

  • IL-10: Control-Refractory *
  • IL-18: Control-Refractory **, V0-Refractory

T cell subtypes

p.adjust.method = "hochberg", method = "wilcox.test", p=0.05

  • V0-Refactory *: ICOS-/Tfh1, ICOS-/Tfh17, Tfh, Tfh17, Tfh2
  • V0-Refactory **: T effect memory-ra, Central memory Tfh, TFH/ICOS-
  • Control-V0 无差异
  • Control-Refractory *: Tfh 17
  • Control-Refractory **: Tfh
  • Control-Refractory ***: Central memory Tfh, TFH/ICOS-
